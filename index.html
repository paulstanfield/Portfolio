About Paul

Hey, I'm Paul, I design and build beautiful experiences for the web, mobile and beyond. I have a BSc (Hons) Degree in Interactive Multimedia Design from the University of Ulster as well as a Foundation Degree in Creative Multimedia from Queens University Belfast. I have experience in both studio, start-up and freelance environments as well as working on a range of projects from web-apps to online stores. I currently work as a Web Designer at Flowlens (Crafty Devil) . My past experience consists of me working for companies such as PlaceSpeak Inc. and Visionworks Interactive, undertaking placement at design studios such as Mette Creates and Life Forms Designs and taking on freelance projects ranging from brand identity to responsive websites. My passion lies in design and front end development although I like to try my hand at other areas and learn something new. When I'm not glued to a screen full of pixels or sketching in a moleskine I can be found delving into t-shirt design, photography and journalism. I'm also an avid football fan, music lover, enjoy a good epic movie or science fiction tv-show and have become a qualified football summer scheme coach in my spare time.
