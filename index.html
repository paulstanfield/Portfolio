    A Bit About Paul

    Paul Stanfield is a designer living and working in Belfast, Northern Ireland. With degrees in Creative Multimedia and Interactive Multimedia Design, he has experience in both studio, startup and freelance environments. His experiences have allowed him to design and develop a range of projects with clients large and small, including Dale Farm, Compass FA, Kiverco and CDE Global. He currently works as a designer for Flowlens as well as running an independent freelance business.

    Paul offers a wide range of services from website design and development that enhances lead generation and engagement to UI/UX design for applications looking an intuitive and organic experience.
