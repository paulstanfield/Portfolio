Bespoke


Vision Carpentry are bespoke fitted furniture and cabinet makers. We specialise in custom built wardrobes, TV and audio units, home offices, kitchens and bathrooms. We are passionate about creating stylish and functional spaces that are designed and tailor-made to each clients requirements through a friendly and professional service.

Experience


We have ten years experience working extensively in London and Belfast. We work primarily on residential projects, taking on a variety of work directly from private clients as well as architects and designers. With our wide range of experience and knowledge, we are happy to design, build and fit any wood products which a client may desire.

Design


Our in-house interior design service can assist you in your complete room design and planning, ensuring that the space works together in a stylish and practical way. We deliver on value by designing products that function properly and are finished to the highest standard using quality woods and wood veneers. We only use high quality ironmongery such as that of Hafele and Blum.
